HOW TO PLAY:

w-a-s-d are used to move the cannon, up-left-down-right respectively,

"spacebar" fires the bullet, the bullet has a costs of 1 shot,
"B" fires the bomb, the bomb has a costs of 3 shot,
"L" fires the laser, the laser has a costs of 7 shot and can be fired only when all the rocks are cleared.

to increase or reduce the power output of the cannon, i.e. the distance of the projectile, press

"M" to increase it 
"N" to reduce it 

the power of the cannon starts from 35 and can go up to 100.

HARD MODE:
the game is the same as in the normal mode but,

the cannon will have a fixed position,

there is a timer of one minute, if you it the target it will be increased of 2 seconds for each hi.


HOW TO WIN:

to win hit the target 20 times,

scoring system and leaderboard: 

from the score will be deducted the bullet fired, so think before you shoot, 
keep in minde that in hard mode the point given are more geneourous, but the deducted are the same.

the best 10 players are gonna be displayed in the leaderboard.
